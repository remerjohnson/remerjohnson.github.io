Hi! My name is Ryan E. Johnson. You are at my new homepage, http://remerjohnson.github.io/. I am a Metadata Librarian at UC San Diego. I make sure digital objects in repositories are described and marked up correctly so that they are discoverable in many ways, including utilizing Linked Data. I also help researchers in Research Data Curation to describe their research. I can be reached at ryan.emerson.johnson@gmail.com.

You can view all this pretty metadata in the UC San Diego Digital Collections.
