My name is Ryan Johnson.I am a Metadata Librarian at [UC San Diego](http://ucsd.edu/). I make sure digital objects in repositories are described and marked up correctly so that they are discoverable in many ways, including utilizing Linked Data. I also help researchers in Research Data Curation to describe their research.
